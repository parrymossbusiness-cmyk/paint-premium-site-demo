Our Story

A Little Rock Painting Company Built on Showing Up

All Metro Painting started in 2003 when Marcus Whitfield, then a one-truck operation working out of Southwest Little Rock, decided the local painting trade needed someone who actually returned phone calls and finished on schedule. Twenty-three years later, that's still the whole business model.

We've painted starter homes in Levy, mid-century ranches in Broadmoor, new construction in Chenal Valley, and office parks along Cantrell Road. Every job gets the same walkthrough, the same written estimate, and the same crew lead accountable from the first drop cloth to the final touch-up. We carry general liability and workers' comp coverage, and we stand behind every finish we apply.
