The All Metro Approach

Exterior Paint Built to Survive Arkansas Weather

Little Rock exteriors take a beating — humid summers, sudden hailstorms, and winter freeze-thaw cycles all work against a paint film that isn't properly prepped. We pressure wash every surface, scrape and sand failing paint back to a sound edge, spot-prime bare wood and rusted metal, and caulk every gap before a sprayer or brush ever touches the house.

We use 100% acrylic exterior coatings rated for the Arkansas climate, and we check the forecast before we schedule — paint applied in the wrong temperature or humidity window fails early, and we'd rather reschedule than cut that corner.

  • Full pressure washing before any prep begins
  • Scraping, sanding, and spot-priming of failing paint
  • Caulking at trim, siding seams, and window frames
  • Landscaping, walkways, and windows fully protected
  • 100% acrylic coatings rated for the Arkansas climate
  • Fascia, soffits, shutters, and doors included
How It Works

Our Exterior Painting Process

Inspection & Estimate

We inspect siding, trim, and paint condition, then provide a written estimate covering every surface.

Wash & Prep

Full pressure washing, scraping, sanding, spot-priming, and caulking before any paint is applied.

Spray & Back-Roll

Acrylic coatings applied by sprayer and back-rolled for full penetration and an even finish.

Detail & Walkthrough

Trim, shutters, and doors are detailed by hand, then we walk the exterior with you before wrapping up.
