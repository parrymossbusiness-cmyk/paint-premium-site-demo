The All Metro Approach

Showroom-Quality Cabinets Without a Full Remodel

Replacing kitchen cabinets can run tens of thousands of dollars and take your kitchen out of commission for weeks. Refinishing gets you a comparable look — new color, new sheen, doors and drawers that close like new — typically in less than a week, at a fraction of the cost.

Every door and drawer front is removed, labeled, and finished in a controlled spray environment for a smooth, brush-mark-free result. Boxes and frames are sprayed in place after full masking of your kitchen. We use catalyzed lacquers and enamels built to resist kitchen heat, grease, and daily handling.

  • Doors and drawers removed, labeled, and spray-finished
  • Cabinet boxes sprayed in place after full kitchen masking
  • Degreasing and sanding for maximum adhesion
  • Catalyzed lacquer or enamel finishes built for kitchens
  • Hardware removed, reinstalled, or upgraded on request
  • Color changes, two-tone finishes, and glaze options
How It Works

Our Cabinet Refinishing Process

Consultation & Color

We assess your cabinets, discuss color and sheen options, and provide a written estimate.

Removal & Prep

Doors and drawers are removed and labeled; all surfaces are degreased and sanded for adhesion.

Spray Finishing

Doors are spray-finished in a controlled setting; boxes are sprayed in place after masking.

Reinstall & Inspect

Hardware and doors are reinstalled, adjusted for a clean reveal, and inspected room by room.
